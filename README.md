AMIR – AI Medical Inference & Research Platform

AMIR is an AI-powered biomedical research platform designed to accelerate scientific discovery by transforming scientific literature into structured, evidence-based knowledge.

Rather than replacing researchers or claiming to discover treatments, AMIR assists scientists by integrating biomedical evidence, constructing knowledge graphs, identifying knowledge gaps, detecting contradictions across studies, and generating research hypotheses for further investigation.

Vision

To become an intelligent research assistant that helps scientists navigate biomedical knowledge and accelerate evidence-driven discovery.

Core Features

- Automated biomedical literature ingestion
- Biomedical entity recognition and normalization
- Knowledge Graph construction
- Evidence scoring and provenance tracking
- Contradiction detection
- Knowledge gap analysis
- Hypothesis generation and ranking
- Cross-disease relationship discovery
- Modular plugin architecture
- Interactive research dashboard

Project Status

AMIR is currently under active development. The platform is evolving toward a comprehensive Research Discovery Platform for biomedical research.

Roadmap

- Data Ingestion Pipeline
- Knowledge Graph Engine
- Evidence Scoring Engine
- Contradiction Engine
- Research Gap Engine
- Hypothesis Generation
